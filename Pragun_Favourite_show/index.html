One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since July 22, 1997, and has been adapted into an anime series produced by Toei Animation since October 20, 1999. The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit. It is praised for its storytelling, world-building, and character development. The author ,Eiichiro Oda has orchestrated this masterpiece with intricate plots and a deep message about freedom, friendship, and adventure.He has redefined justice and morality through his characters and their journeys.
