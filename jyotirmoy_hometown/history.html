From the mythical past to the modern-day metropolis.

Pragjyotishpura (Ancient Era)

Guwahati finds its earliest mentions in the epic Mahabharata, where it was the capital of the mythical Naraka and Bhagadatta. It was known as Pragjyotishpura, the "City of Eastern Light" or "Astrology", suggesting a center for astronomical studies.

Ahom and Mughal Conflicts (Late Medieval)

The city was a frequent battleground between the Ahom Kingdom and the Mughal Empire. The famous Battle of Saraighat in 1671, fought near Guwahati, saw the Ahom general Lachit Borphukan decisively defeat the Mughal forces, securing the region for the Ahoms.
