Rosa Diaz

-Played by Stephanie Beatriz

About Rosa

Rosa Diaz is a tough and mysterious detective at the 99th precinct. She's intimidating and keeps her personal life very private. Nobody knows much about her background, and she likes it that way.

Personality

Rosa is strong, independent, and doesn't show emotions easily. She's incredibly loyal to her friends but scary to everyone else. She loves motorcycles and has many hidden talents and mysterious hobbies.

Best Moments

Rosa's best moments include her coming out storyline, her secret ballet dancing, protecting her squad, and her tough-but-caring friendship with everyone. She's also great at interrogations.

Fun Facts

  • She rides a motorcycle
  • She came out as bisexual in Season 5
  • Her real name might not even be Rosa Diaz
