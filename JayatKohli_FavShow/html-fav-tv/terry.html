Terry Jeffords

-Played by Terry Crews

About Terry

Terry Jeffords is the sergeant at the 99th precinct. He's strong, caring, and refers to himself in third person. Terry is a family man who loves his wife and twin daughters more than anything.

Personality

Terry is kind, supportive, and emotional. He loves yogurt and working out. Despite his big muscles, he's sensitive and cares deeply about everyone's feelings. He's like a father figure to the squad.

Best Moments

Terry's best moments include his love for yogurt, his Terry-isms, becoming a lieutenant, and his emotional speeches. He's also known for his artistic side and love for his family.

Fun Facts

  • He refers to himself as "Terry"
  • He loves yogurt more than life
