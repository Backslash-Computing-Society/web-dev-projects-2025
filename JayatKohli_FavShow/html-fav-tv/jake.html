Jake Peralta

-Played by Andy Samberg

About Jake

Jake Peralta is the main character of Brooklyn Nine-Nine. He's a talented detective who doesn't take things too seriously. Jake loves movies, especially Die Hard, and often makes pop culture references. He's immature but very good at his job.

Personality

Jake is funny, laid-back, and competitive. He loves pranks and games, especially the annual Halloween Heist. Despite his childish behavior, he truly cares about his friends and always tries to do the right thing.

Best Moments

Some of Jake's best moments include winning multiple Halloween Heists, going undercover in prison, and his proposal to Amy. He's also known for his amazing friendship with Charles Boyle.

Fun Facts

  • His favorite movie is Die Hard
  • He married Amy Santiago in Season 5
  • He has a pet turtle named Peanut Butter
