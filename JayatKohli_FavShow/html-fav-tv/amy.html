Amy Santiago

-Played by Melissa Fumero

About Amy

Amy Santiago is an organized and ambitious detective at the 99th precinct. She's a perfectionist who loves rules, binders, and organization. Amy is very competitive and dreams of becoming captain one day.

Personality

Amy is hardworking, responsible, and a little uptight. She loves making lists and color-coding everything. She's extremely loyal to her friends and Captain Holt, who she sees as a mentor.

Best Moments

Amy's best moments include winning Halloween Heists, her relationship with Jake, becoming a sergeant, and her hilarious competitive streak. She's also known for her famous "binder system."

Fun Facts

  • She has 7 brothers
  • She married Jake Peralta in Season 5
  • She loves binders and organization
