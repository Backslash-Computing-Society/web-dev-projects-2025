Charles Boyle

-Played by Joe Lo Truglio

About Charles

Charles Boyle is Jake's best friend and a detective at the 99th precinct. He's enthusiastic, loyal, and a little awkward. Charles is a foodie who loves trying new restaurants and cooking fancy meals.

Personality

Charles is kind, supportive, and extremely loyal to Jake. He's emotional and sometimes too intense, but he always means well. He's obsessed with food and loves talking about fancy ingredients and recipes.

Best Moments

Charles's best moments include his unwavering friendship with Jake, his marriage to Genevieve, adopting his son Nikolaj, and his food adventures. He's also surprisingly brave when protecting his friends.

Fun Facts

  • He's Jake's best friend and biggest supporter
  • He adopted a son named Nikolaj
  • He's a food expert and loves cooking
