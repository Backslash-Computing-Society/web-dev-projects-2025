Captain Raymond Holt

-Played by Andre Braugher

About Captain Holt

Raymond Holt is the captain of the 99th precinct. He's stoic, professional, and speaks in a very formal way. Despite his serious personality, he deeply cares about his team and has a dry sense of humor.

Personality

Captain Holt is calm, intelligent, and rarely shows emotion. He loves classical music, proper grammar, and his corgi Cheddar. He's also a strong leader who fought against discrimination in the police force.

Best Moments

Holt's best moments include his love for Cheddar, his competitions with Jake, teaching life lessons to the squad, and his funny attempts at being "fun." His monotone delivery makes everything hilarious.

Fun Facts

  • He's married to Kevin Cozner
  • He loves his dog Cheddar more than anything
  • He's a history buff and wine enthusiast
