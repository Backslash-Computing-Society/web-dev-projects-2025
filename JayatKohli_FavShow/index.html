About the Show

Brooklyn Nine-Nine is an American police comedy series that follows the detectives of the 99th precinct in Brooklyn. The show combines hilarious humor with heartwarming moments and great friendships. It aired from 2013 to 2021 and has 8 amazing seasons.

The Story

The show follows Detective Jake Peralta and his colleagues at Brooklyn's 99th precinct. When a new captain, Raymond Holt, takes over, Jake's carefree approach to police work is challenged. Together with Amy, Rosa, Charles, Terry, Gina, and Hitchcock and Scully, they solve crimes while creating chaos and unforgettable moments. From Halloween heists to wedding planning, the squad becomes a second family.

Fun Facts
